This section provides background on \acf{av}.
We begin by outlining the historical roots in \Cref{sec:origin}, tracing early efforts in \ac{aa} and the emergence of statistical methods, with particular attention to stylometry in \Cref{sec:stylometry}.
Next, we highlight the enduring relevance of \ac{av} in contemporary research and applications in \Cref{sec:digital_forensics}.
Following this, we present the technical background necessary to understand current methodologies in \Cref{sec:technical_background}, outline the key tasks that define the field in \Cref{sec:tasks}, and review a selection of \acl{sota} approaches in \Cref{sec:sota_methods}.
Finally, \Cref{sec:llm_impact} examines how recent advances in \ac{llm} development are reshaping the landscape of \ac{av} and its future prospects.
